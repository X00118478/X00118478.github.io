About

I am a Fourth Year Computing Student in the Institute of Technology Tallaght Dublin.
I have a passionate interest in Computing, event managing and sound engineering. I have gained a lot of experience and skills over the years working for companies such as the Deparmtment of Social Protection, Aviva Stadium, Áras Chrónáin, Xilinx, Irish Red Cross, Davis Events Agency. ​
More recently I have worked on Albums, a Movie Soundtrack and Promotional Videos for Foras Na Gaeilge. ​

I began my journey by presenting on West Dublin Access Radio 96fm there I trained in all aspects of digital media productions. From this learning, I managed and produced a weekly radio show every Saturday and Tuesday on WDAR.
From that weekly production with WDAR 96FM I then moved to Raidió Na Life 106.4FM and produced and presented a show which aired live four times weekly. After that I progressed to RTÉ Raidió Na Gaelteachta and Christmas FM. I have produced Stingers(Advertisements) for Spin 103.8 FM.

When in College I wanted to contribute towards student life so my friends and I founded the ITTD Cumann Gaelach. In the foundation year the society won the B.I.C.S Best New Society Award 2016 and Best New Society in the Institute of Technology Tallaght.
In my final year of studies I have contributed further towards the college by tutoring students in disadvantaged schools.I teach Higher/Ordinary Irish for the Junior Certificate and for the Leaving Certificate.
